**The "Director" vs. "Employee" Hurdle**
For this couple moving from Sydney to Barcelona, the main obstacle was their status. They paid themselves a salary, but because they owned >25% of the company, Spanish Immigration (UGE) views this as a "Professional Relationship," not an employment one. Filing as standard employees would have led to an immediate denial (Denegado).

**The Result: Family Synchronization**
The application included both children (ages 6 and 9). By filing electronically through Madrid, we secured the approval in **20 business days**.
