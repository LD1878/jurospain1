How is this calculated?

The Spanish government uses the IPREM (Public Income Indicator of Multiple Effects) to set visa thresholds.

  • Main Applicant: 200% of IPREM (~€2,646)
  • Spouse: +75% of IPREM (~€992)
  • Per Child: +25% of IPREM (~€331)

*Figures based on estimated 2026 IPREM of €600/month (14 payments) pro-rated. Final amounts may vary slightly by consulate.
