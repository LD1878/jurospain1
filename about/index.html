The Origin Story

JURO Spain was launched after our founder experienced the chaos of Spanish bureaucracy firsthand.

The inconsistent information from government offices. The lawyers who wouldn't reply for weeks. The hidden fees that appeared at the last minute. The process was broken.

We realized a simple truth: Great legal outcomes require great process management. Lawyers are experts at the law, but they aren't always experts at customer service, speed, or logistics. That is where we come in.

Common Questions About Us

Are you a law firm?

JURO Spain is a legal concierge and relocation consultancy. For reserved legal activities (like court representation or final legal opinions), we rely on our vetted network of licensed Spanish attorneys (*Abogados*). This model gives you the best of both worlds: dedicated project management and regulated legal safety.

Do I need to be in Spain to start?

No. 90% of our clients start their process from abroad (UK, USA, Canada, etc.). We handle everything digitally and via Power of Attorney (POA), so you only need to travel when absolutely necessary.

Why are your prices lower than traditional firms?

Because we are efficient. Traditional firms bill by the hour and use antiquated processes. We use fixed fees and modern technology to streamline applications, passing the savings directly to you.
