The JURO Spain Story

We Built the Service
We Wish We Had.

Moving to Spain should be the adventure of a lifetime, not a paperwork nightmare.

We are the bridge between your relocation goals and Spanish bureaucracy.

Born from Frustration.

JURO Spain was launched after our founding team experienced the chaos of Spanish immigration firsthand. We saw the inconsistent information from government offices, the lawyers who wouldn't reply for weeks, and the hidden "Gestoría" fees that appeared at the last minute.

"Great legal outcomes require great project management. Traditional law firms focus on the code; we focus on the client."

We realized that while Spanish attorneys are experts at the law, the logistics of moving—sworn translations, bank accounts, TIE appointments, and constant communication—often fall through the cracks. We built JURO to fix the process.

Common Questions

Are you a law firm?

JURO Spain is a legal concierge and relocation consultancy. For reserved legal activities (such as formal representation in court or specialized legal opinions), we work exclusively with our vetted network of registered Spanish attorneys (Abogados). This ensures you get dedicated support and regulated legal safety.

Why use JURO instead of a direct lawyer?

Speed and communication. Most lawyers are busy in court and don't have the resources to guide you through everyday logistics like bank accounts or finding a translator. We provide a 100% English-speaking interface and project manage the lawyer for you, ensuring your file is processed as fast as possible.

How do you verify your partner lawyers?

Every attorney in our network is fully insured, registered with their local Colegio de Abogados, and has a proven track record of successful expat residency applications. We only work with professionals who share our commitment to fixed fees and digital efficiency.
