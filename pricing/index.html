Pricing FAQ

Are there extra lawyer fees on top of these prices?
No. Our fees include the professional fees for the registered attorneys in our legal network who handle your formal submission. You only pay for government taxes (Tasas) and external sworn translations.
When do I need to make payment?
We work on a 50/50 basis for most residency services: 50% to begin the document audit and case preparation, and 50% upon the successful submission of your application to the authorities.
Do you offer family discounts?
Absolutely. Family applications (spouse and children) are significantly cheaper when processed alongside the main applicant. We provide custom quotes for families to ensure the best possible value.
What if my application is rejected?
We perform a strict internal eligibility audit before taking your case. If we don't think you'll pass, we'll tell you before you pay. If a rejection occurs due to our error, we offer a full re-submission at no extra cost.
