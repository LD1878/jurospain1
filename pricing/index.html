Pricing Questions

Are there extra lawyer fees on top of these prices?

No. Our fixed fees include the professional fees for the registered attorneys in our legal network who handle your formal submission. The only extras you pay are government taxes (Tasas) and external sworn translations.

When do I pay?

We typically work on a 50/50 split: 50% upfront to start the document audit and 50% upon successful submission of your file to the Spanish authorities.

Do you offer discounts for families?

Yes. For family applications (spouses and children), we offer significantly reduced rates for additional dependents. Contact us for a custom family relocation quote.
