<div class="glossary-section">

    <details style="margin-bottom: 1rem; border: 1px solid #ddd; border-left: 5px solid #b31b1b; padding-left: 15px; background-color: #f9f9f9;">
        <summary style="font-weight: 700; font-size: 1.25rem; cursor: pointer; color: #003366; padding: 10px 0;">Agencia Tributaria (Hacienda)</summary>
        <p style="margin-top: 0.5rem; padding-bottom: 10px;">
            <span style="font-weight: 600;">Definition:</span> The **Spanish Tax Agency**, often colloquially called *Hacienda*. This is the central government body responsible for collecting state taxes and enforcing **fiscal compliance** across residents and non-residents.
        </p>
    </details>
    <details style="margin-bottom: 1rem; border: 1px solid #ddd; border-left: 5px solid #b31b1b; padding-left: 15px; background-color: #f9f9f9;">
        <summary style="font-weight: 700; font-size: 1.25rem; cursor: pointer; color: #003366; padding: 10px 0;">IRPF (Impuesto sobre la Renta de las Personas F√≠sicas)</summary>
        <p style="margin-top: 0.5rem; padding-bottom: 10px;">
            <span style="font-weight: 600;">Definition:</span> **Personal Income Tax**. The standard tax residents in Spain pay on their **worldwide income**. Tax residency is triggered by spending **more than 183 days** per year in Spain. This is a progressive tax. <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Personal_income_tax_in_Spain" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" class="glossary-link">IRPF on Wikipedia.</a>
        </p>
    </details>
    <details style="margin-bottom: 1rem; border: 1px solid #ddd; border-left: 5px solid #b31b1b; padding-left: 15px; background-color: #f9f9f9;">
        <summary style="font-weight: 700; font-size: 1.25rem; cursor: pointer; color: #003366; padding: 10px 0;">IRNR (Impuesto sobre la Renta de No Residentes)</summary>
        <p style="margin-top: 0.5rem; padding-bottom: 10px;">
            <span style="font-weight: 600;">Definition:</span> **Non-Resident Income Tax**. This flat-rate tax is paid by individuals who are **not tax residents** but earn income from specific Spanish sources, most commonly from **imputed income** on property they own, or from rental income.
        </p>
    </details>
    <details style="margin-bottom: 1rem; border: 1px solid #ddd; border-left: 5px solid #b31b1b; padding-left: 15px; background-color: #f9f9f9;">
        <summary style="font-weight: 700; font-size: 1.25rem; cursor: pointer; color: #003366; padding: 10px 0;">R√©gimen de Impatriados (Beckham Law)</summary>
        <p style="margin-top: 0.5rem; padding-bottom: 10px;">
            <span style="font-weight: 600;">Definition:</span> A **Special Expat Tax Regime** that allows qualified foreigners moving to Spain (e.g., DNV holders) to choose to be taxed as a non-resident for up to six years, paying a flat **24% rate** on Spanish-sourced income, instead of the higher, progressive IRPF rates. <a href="{{ '/blog/spain-beckham-law-tax-guide-2025/' | relative_url }}" class="glossary-link">Beckham Law Guide.</a>
        </p>
    </details>
    <details style="margin-bottom: 1rem; border: 1px solid #ddd; border-left: 5px solid #b31b1b; padding-left: 15px; background-color: #f9f9f9;">
        <summary style="font-weight: 700; font-size: 1.25rem; cursor: pointer; color: #003366; padding: 10px 0;">Modelo 720</summary>
        <p style="margin-top: 0.5rem; padding-bottom: 10px;">
            <span style="font-weight: 600;">Definition:</span> The mandatory informative declaration form used by **Spanish tax residents** to declare **overseas assets** (property, bank accounts, investments) valued at over ‚Ç¨50,000. Penalties for non-compliance are severe.
        </p>
    </details>
    <details style="margin-bottom: 1rem; border: 1px solid #ddd; border-left: 5px solid #b31b1b; padding-left: 15px; background-color: #f9f9f9;">
        <summary style="font-weight: 700; font-size: 1.25rem; cursor: pointer; color: #003366; padding: 10px 0;">Impuesto sobre el Patrimonio</summary>
        <p style="margin-top: 0.5rem; padding-bottom: 10px;">
            <span style="font-weight: 600;">Definition:</span> **Wealth Tax**. An annual tax levied on a tax resident's net worldwide assets (assets minus debts). The minimum exemption thresholds and rates are set by the **Autonomous Communities**.
        </p>
    </details>
    <details style="margin-bottom: 1rem; border: 1px solid #ddd; border-left: 5px solid #b31b1b; padding-left: 15px; background-color: #f9f9f9;">
        <summary style="font-weight: 700; font-size: 1.25rem; cursor: pointer; color: #003366; padding: 10px 0;">Impuesto de Sucesiones y Donaciones (ISD)</summary>
        <p style="margin-top: 0.5rem; padding-bottom: 10px;">
            <span style="font-weight: 600;">Definition:</span> **Inheritance and Gift Tax**. A tax on assets received through inheritance (succession) or as a gift (donation). This tax is heavily regulated by the **Autonomous Communities**, leading to massive differences in liability across Spain. <a href="{{ '/blog/spain-inheritance-tax-for-expats/' | relative_url }}" class="glossary-link">Inheritance Tax Guide.</a>
        </p>
    </details>
    <details style="margin-bottom: 1rem; border: 1px solid #ddd; border-left: 5px solid #b31b1b; padding-left: 15px; background-color: #f9f9f9;">
        <summary style="font-weight: 700; font-size: 1.25rem; cursor: pointer; color: #003366; padding: 10px 0;">ITP (Impuesto de Transmisiones Patrimoniales)</summary>
        <p style="margin-top: 0.5rem; padding-bottom: 10px;">
            <span style="font-weight: 600;">Definition:</span> **Property Transfer Tax**. A regional tax paid by the buyer when acquiring a **resale** (second-hand) property. It is one of the largest single costs of purchasing a Spanish home, replacing IVA/AJD.
        </p>
    </details>
    <details style="margin-bottom: 1rem; border: 1px solid #ddd; border-left: 5px solid #b31b1b; padding-left: 15px; background-color: #f9f9f9;">
        <summary style="font-weight: 700; font-size: 1.25rem; cursor: pointer; color: #003366; padding: 10px 0;">IVA (Impuesto sobre el Valor A√±adido)</summary>
        <p style="margin-top: 0.5rem; padding-bottom: 10px;">
            <span style="font-weight: 600;">Definition:</span> **Value Added Tax (VAT)**. The sales tax paid on most goods and services, as well as on the purchase of a **new build** property (in which case it replaces ITP).
        </p>
    </details>
    <details style="margin-bottom: 1rem; border: 1px solid #ddd; border-left: 5px solid #b31b1b; padding-left: 15px; background-color: #f9f9f9;">
        <summary style="font-weight: 700; font-size: 1.25rem; cursor: pointer; color: #003366; padding: 10px 0;">AJD (Actos Jur√≠dicos Documentados)</summary>
        <p style="margin-top: 0.5rem; padding-bottom: 10px;">
            <span style="font-weight: 600;">Definition:</span> **Stamp Duty** or **Tax on Documented Legal Acts**. A regional tax paid on notarized documents, primarily applied to the signing of a mortgage deed and often to the initial purchase of a new build property.
        </p>
    </details>
    <details style="margin-bottom: 1rem; border: 1px solid #ddd; border-left: 5px solid #b31b1b; padding-left: 15px; background-color: #f9f9f9;">
        <summary style="font-weight: 700; font-size: 1.25rem; cursor: pointer; color: #003366; padding: 10px 0;">Declaraci√≥n de la Renta</summary>
        <p style="margin-top: 0.5rem; padding-bottom: 10px;">
            <span style="font-weight: 600;">Definition:</span> The annual **Income Tax Return** that must be filed with the *Agencia Tributaria* by all tax residents of Spain, consolidating all worldwide income for the previous year.
        </p>
    </details>
    <details style="margin-bottom: 1rem; border: 1px solid #ddd; border-left: 5px solid #b31b1b; padding-left: 15px; background-color: #f9f9f9;">
        <summary style="font-weight: 700; font-size: 1.25rem; cursor: pointer; color: #003366; padding: 10px 0;">NIF (N√∫mero de Identificaci√≥n Fiscal)</summary>
        <p style="margin-top: 0.5rem; padding-bottom: 10px;">
            <span style="font-weight: 600;">Definition:</span> The **Fiscal Identification Number**. This is the generic term for the tax ID. For foreigners, this is the NIE; for Spanish nationals, it is their DNI.
        </p>
    </details>
    <details style="margin-bottom: 1rem; border: 1px solid #ddd; border-left: 5px solid #b31b1b; padding-left: 15px; background-color: #f9f9f9;">
        <summary style="font-weight: 700; font-size: 1.25rem; cursor: pointer; color: #003366; padding: 10px 0;">Cuenta Bancaria No Residente</summary>
        <p style="margin-top: 0.5rem; padding-bottom: 10px;">
            <span style="font-weight: 600;">Definition:</span> A **Non-Resident Bank Account**. A specific type of bank account legally required for non-residents purchasing property in Spain, often converted to a resident account once the TIE is issued.
        </p>
    </details>
</div>

## ‚úÖ 4. Bureaucracy & Administration Terms

<div class="glossary-section">

    <details style="margin-bottom: 1rem; border: 1px solid #ddd; border-left: 5px solid #b31b1b; padding-left: 15px; background-color: #f9f9f9;">
        <summary style="font-weight: 700; font-size: 1.25rem; cursor: pointer; color: #003366; padding: 10px 0;">Empadronamiento (Padr√≥n)</summary>
        <p style="margin-top: 0.5rem; padding-bottom: 10px;">
            <span style="font-weight: 600;">Definition:</span> The mandatory process of officially **registering yourself with the Census** at your residential address via the local *Ayuntamiento* (Town Hall). This document is essential for many administrative tasks and proving residency. <a href="{{ '/blog/empadronamiento-in-spain-complete-2026-registration-guide/' | relative_url }}" class="glossary-link">Empadronamiento Guide.</a>
        </p>
    </details>
    <details style="margin-bottom: 1rem; border: 1px solid #ddd; border-left: 5px solid #b31b1b; padding-left: 15px; background-color: #f9f9f9;">
        <summary style="font-weight: 700; font-size: 1.25rem; cursor: pointer; color: #003366; padding: 10px 0;">Ayuntamiento</summary>
        <p style="margin-top: 0.5rem; padding-bottom: 10px;">
            <span style="font-weight: 600;">Definition:</span> The **Town Hall** or Municipal Government. This is the local administrative body responsible for municipal services, property taxes (IBI), and the *Empadronamiento*.

    <details style="margin-bottom: 1rem; border: 1px solid #ddd; border-left: 5px solid #b31b1b; padding-left: 15px; background-color: #f9f9f9;">

        <p style="margin-top: 0.5rem; padding-bottom: 10px;">
            <span style="font-weight: 600;">Definition:</span> A **Digital Certificate** (similar to an electronic passport) that allows residents to securely **identify themselves online** and digitally sign documents with all Spanish government agencies, often eliminating the need for in-person appointments. <a href="https://www.cert.fnmt.es/certificados" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" class="glossary-link">Official FNMT Certificate Site.</a>

        <p style="margin-top: 0.5rem; padding-bottom: 10px;">
            <span style="font-weight: 600;">Definition:</span> The term for a **Mandatory Pre-booked Appointment**. Most Spanish government offices (including the *Polic√≠a Nacional*, *Hacienda*, and *Extranjer√≠a*) require you to secure an appointment online before attending.
        </p>
    </details>
    <details style="margin-bottom: 1rem; border: 1px solid #ddd; border-left: 5px solid #b31b1b; padding-left: 15px; background-color: #f9f9f9;">

        <p style="margin-top: 0.5rem; padding-bottom: 10px;">
            <span style="font-weight: 600;">Definition:</span> A **Sworn Translator**. An official, government-certified translator whose work carries the same legal validity as the original document. Required for nearly all foreign official documents (e.g., birth certificates, marriage certificates) submitted for Spanish procedures.
        </p>
    </details>
    <details style="margin-bottom: 1rem; border: 1px solid #ddd; border-left: 5px solid #b31b1b; padding-left: 15px; background-color: #f9f9f9;">
        <summary style="font-weight: 700; font-size: 1.25rem; cursor: pointer; color: #003366; padding: 10px 0;">Seguridad Social</summary>
        <p style="margin-top: 0.5rem; padding-bottom: 10px;">
            <span style="font-weight: 600;">Definition:</span> **Social Security**. The government body managing public health care, pensions, and social benefits. Registration is required for all workers and residents applying for public services.
        </p>
    </details>
    <details style="margin-bottom: 1rem; border: 1px solid #ddd; border-left: 5px solid #b31b1b; padding-left: 15px; background-color: #f9f9f9;">
        <summary style="font-weight: 700; font-size: 1.25rem; cursor: pointer; color: #003366; padding: 10px 0;">N√∫mero de Afiliaci√≥n (NAF)</summary>
        <p style="margin-top: 0.5rem; padding-bottom: 10px;">
            <span style="font-weight: 600;">Definition:</span> Your **Social Security Affiliation Number**, which is the unique identification number required to register for **public health care** or begin employment/register as *Aut√≥nomo*.
        </p>
    </details>
    <details style="margin-bottom: 1rem; border: 1px solid #ddd; border-left: 5px solid #b31b1b; padding-left: 15px; background-color: #f9f9f9;">
        <summary style="font-weight: 700; font-size: 1.25rem; cursor: pointer; color: #003366; padding: 10px 0;">Pensi√≥n No Contributiva</summary>
        <p style="margin-top: 0.5rem; padding-bottom: 10px;">
            <span style="font-weight: 600;">Definition:</span> A **Non-Contributory Pension** granted to residents who have not paid sufficient contributions into the system but meet strict income and residency requirements.
        </p>
    </details>
    <details style="margin-bottom: 1rem; border: 1px solid #ddd; border-left: 5px solid #b31b1b; padding-left: 15px; background-color: #f9f9f9;">
        <summary style="font-weight: 700; font-size: 1.25rem; cursor: pointer; color: #003366; padding: 10px 0;">DNI (Documento Nacional de Identidad)</summary>
        <p style="margin-top: 0.5rem; padding-bottom: 10px;">

        </p>
    </details>
    <details style="margin-bottom: 1rem; border: 1px solid #ddd; border-left: 5px solid #b31b1b; padding-left: 15px; background-color: #f9f9f9;">
        <summary style="font-weight: 700; font-size: 1.25rem; cursor: pointer; color: #003366; padding: 10px 0;">Volante de Empadronamiento</summary>
        <p style="margin-top: 0.5rem; padding-bottom: 10px;">
            <span style="font-weight: 600;">Definition:</span> A simpler, shorter version of the *Certificado de Empadronamiento* used for internal administrative processes that require proof of local residence.
        </p>
    </details>
    <details style="margin-bottom: 1rem; border: 1px solid #ddd; border-left: 5px solid #b31b1b; padding-left: 15px; background-color: #f9f9f9;">

        <p style="margin-top: 0.5rem; padding-bottom: 10px;">
            <span style="font-weight: 600;">Definition:</span> The **Hague Apostille**. A certification stamp required to legalize a foreign public document (e.g., birth certificate, police check) for use in Spain, provided both countries are parties to the Hague Convention.

    </details>
    <details style="margin-bottom: 1rem; border: 1px solid #ddd; border-left: 5px solid #b31b1b; padding-left: 15px; background-color: #f9f9f9;">

        <p style="margin-top: 0.5rem; padding-bottom: 10px;">
            <span style="font-weight: 600;">Definition:</span> The **Family Book**. A record provided by the Civil Registry detailing marriages, births, and deaths within a family unit.
        </p>
    </details>
    <details style="margin-bottom: 1rem; border: 1px solid #ddd; border-left: 5px solid #b31b1b; padding-left: 15px; background-color: #f9f9f9;">
        <summary style="font-weight: 700; font-size: 1.25rem; cursor: pointer; color: #003366; padding: 10px 0;">Certificado de Nacimiento</summary>
        <p style="margin-top: 0.5rem; padding-bottom: 10px;">
            <span style="font-weight: 600;">Definition:</span> **Birth Certificate**. This document, often required in an official **Sworn Translation** (*Traducci√≥n Jurada*), is fundamental for proving identity and lineage during residency applications.
        </p>
