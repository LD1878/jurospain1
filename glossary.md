<h1>Spanish Legal & Immigration Glossary</h1>
<p style="text-align:center;">Quick-reference guide to Spanish legal, visa, and immigration terminology — explained in plain English for expats.</p>

<!-- Section A -->
<h2>A</h2>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Abogado</summary>
  <p>Lawyer or attorney. Required for property purchases and complex immigration matters. <a class="cta" href="/services/">See our services</a></p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> AJD (Actos Jurídicos Documentados)</summary>
  <p>Stamp duty tax applied to legal documents like mortgages. <a class="cta" href="/pricing/">Check related pricing</a></p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Apostille</summary>
  <p>International certificate authenticating documents for use abroad. <a class="cta" href="/services/">We handle apostille and notarisation</a></p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Autónomo</summary>
  <p>Self-employed registration with Social Security. <a class="cta" href="/services/#autonomo-registration">Learn more</a></p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Ayuntamiento</summary>
  <p>Town hall for empadronamiento and local admin. <a class="cta" href="/services/#empadronamiento">See empadronamiento service</a></p>
</details>

<!-- Section B -->
<h2>B</h2>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Beckham Law (Ley Beckham)</summary>
  <p>Special tax regime for new residents paying 24% flat tax for up to 6 years.</p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> BOE (Boletín Oficial del Estado)</summary>
  <p>Official state gazette where laws and announcements are published.</p>
</details>

<!-- Section C -->
<h2>C</h2>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Catastro</summary>
  <p>Land registry for property records. <a class="cta" href="/services/#property-transactions">Learn about property services</a></p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Cédula de Habitabilidad</summary>
  <p>Certificate confirming property meets habitability standards.</p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Certificado de Empadronamiento</summary>
  <p>Proof of registered address. <a class="cta" href="/services/#empadronamiento">See empadronamiento support</a></p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Certificado Digital</summary>
  <p>Digital certificate for official online business with Spanish government.</p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> CCSE Exam</summary>
  <p>Constitutional & sociocultural knowledge test required for permanent residency and citizenship.</p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Comunidad de Propietarios</summary>
  <p>Homeowners' association managing shared areas and fees.</p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Consejería</summary>
  <p>Regional government department responsible for immigration offices.</p>
</details>

<!-- Section D -->
<h2>D</h2>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> DELE</summary>
  <p>Official Spanish language certificate. A2 for permanent residency; B1 for citizenship.</p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Digital Nomad Visa</summary>
  <p>Permit for remote workers. <a class="cta" href="/services/#digital-nomad-visa">See Digital Nomad Visa support</a></p>
</details>

<!-- Section E -->
<h2>E</h2>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Empadronamiento</summary>
  <p>Register your residence at town hall. <a class="cta" href="/services/#empadronamiento">Learn more</a></p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Escritura Pública</summary>
  <p>Public deed notarized for property purchase. <a class="cta" href="/services/#buying-property">See property services</a></p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> EX-14 / EX-15 / EX-17 Forms</summary>
  <p>Application forms for Digital Nomad Visa, NIE, and TIE.</p>
</details>

<!-- Section F -->
<h2>F</h2>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Finca</summary>
  <p>Rural property or estate.</p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Foreigners' Office (Oficina de Extranjería)</summary>
  <p>Immigration office handling residence permits and renewals.</p>
</details>

<!-- Section G -->
<h2>G</h2>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Gastos de Comunidad</summary>
  <p>Community fees for apartment maintenance.</p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Gestor/Gestoría</summary>
  <p>Administrative agent handling taxes, business registrations, and bureaucracy.</p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Golden Visa</summary>
  <p>Former residency-by-investment program (ended April 2025).</p>
</details>

<!-- Section I -->
<h2>I</h2>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> IBI (Impuesto sobre Bienes Inmuebles)</summary>
  <p>Annual property tax paid to local municipalities.</p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> IPREM</summary>
  <p>Income indicator for visa eligibility and minimum income requirements.</p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> IRNR</summary>
  <p>Non-resident income tax. Must file if you own Spanish property.</p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> ITP</summary>
  <p>Transfer tax on resale property. Varies by region.</p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> IVA</summary>
  <p>Value-added tax. 10% for new properties.</p>
</details>

<!-- Section L -->
<h2>L</h2>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Ley de Startups</summary>
  <p>Law introducing Digital Nomad Visa and incentives for entrepreneurs.</p>
</details>

<!-- Section M -->
<h2>M</h2>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Modelo 790 Código 012</summary>
  <p>Fee payment form for NIE applications. €9.84. Paid at Spanish bank.</p>
</details>

<!-- Section N -->
<h2>N</h2>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> NIE</summary>
  <p>Foreigner Identification Number, essential for banking, property, employment. <a class="cta" href="/services/#nie">See NIE support</a></p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> NIF</summary>
  <p>Tax identification number; same as NIE for foreigners.</p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Non-Lucrative Visa</summary>
  <p>Permit for financially independent individuals. <a class="cta" href="/services/#non-lucrative-visa">See Non-Lucrative Visa support</a></p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Nota Simple</summary>
  <p>Land Registry extract showing ownership, mortgages, and charges.</p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Notario</summary>
  <p>Public official who authenticates legal documents and property transactions.</p>
</details>

<!-- Section O -->
<h2>O</h2>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Obra Nueva</summary>
  <p>New-build property sold directly by a developer.</p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Oficina de Extranjería</summary>
  <p>See Foreigners' Office.</p>
</details>

<!-- Section P -->
<h2>P</h2>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Padrón Municipal</summary>
  <p>Municipal register of residents. <a class="cta" href="/services/#empadronamiento">See empadronamiento support</a></p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Permiso de Residencia</summary>
  <p>Residence permit to live legally in Spain.</p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Plusvalía Municipal</summary>
  <p>Capital gains tax on land/property value increase.</p>
</details>

<!-- Section R -->
<h2>R</h2>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Registro de la Propiedad</summary>
  <p>Land Registry recording property ownership, mortgages, and charges. <a class="cta" href="/services/#property-transactions">Learn about property services</a></p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Residencia</summary>
  <p>Residence or residency status in Spain.</p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Resguardo</summary>
  <p>Proof of submission for administrative applications.</p>
</details>

<!-- Section S -->
<h2>S</h2>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Schengen Area</summary>
  <p>26 European countries with open borders. Spanish residency allows travel here.</p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Sede Electrónica</summary>
  <p>Government websites for appointments, online procedures, and document submission.</p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Social Security Number</summary>
  <p>Required for employment and healthcare access. Different from NIE.</p>
</details>

<!-- Section T -->
<h2>T</h2>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> TIE</summary>
  <p>Foreigner Identity Card for non-EU residents. Must carry as ID.</p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Traductor Jurado</summary>
  <p>Sworn translator for certified legal document translations.</p>
</details>

<!-- Section V -->
<h2>V</h2>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Visa Nacional</summary>
  <p>National long-stay visa for work, study, non-lucrative, or digital nomad purposes.</p>
</details>
<details>
  <summary><span class="arrow">►</span> Volante de Empadronamiento</summary>
  <p>See Certificado de Empadronamiento.</p>
</details>
