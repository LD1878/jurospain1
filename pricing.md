## Frequently Asked Questions

<details>
<summary>What is an NIE and why do I need it?</summary>
<p>The NIE is your Spanish Foreigner Identification Number. It is required for almost any official transaction in Spain including opening a bank account, buying property, or starting a business. Our NIE Fast-Track package simplifies the process.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>How long does it take to get my NIE?</summary>
<p>With our full support, most clients receive their NIE within a few weeks depending on appointment availability and document preparation.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>What is included in property purchase support?</summary>
<p>We cover contract review, checking property registration, notary representation, and post-purchase filings to make your property purchase in Spain secure and stress-free.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Who should use the Digital Nomad Visa Support service?</summary>
<p>Anyone planning to live and work in Spain under a Digital Nomad Visa will benefit. We guide you through eligibility, income documentation, taxes, and residency activation.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>What is a Non-Lucrative Visa and who is it for?</summary>
<p>A Non-Lucrative Visa allows you to live in Spain without working locally. It is ideal for retirees, remote workers, or people with passive income. We assist with financial planning, insurance, consulate preparation, and renewals.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I pay in installments?</summary>
<p>Yes, we split the fixed fee 50/50. You pay half to start and the remainder upon completion.</p>
</details>
