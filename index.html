Common Questions

How much income do I need for the Digital Nomad Visa in 2026?

To qualify for the DNV, you must prove a monthly income of 200% of the SMI (Minimum Interprofessional Wage). In 2026, this threshold is approximately €2,700 per month for a single applicant.

Can I apply if I am already in Spain as a tourist?

Yes. Applying from within Spain (during your 90-day tourist window) allows us to file directly for the 3-Year Residency Permit. If you apply from a Consulate, you only receive a 1-Year Visa.

Does JuroSpain handle the "Beckham Law" application?

Yes. The "Special Expats Regime" allows you to pay a flat 24% tax rate. You must apply within 6 months of arriving. We usually file this immediately after your visa is approved.
