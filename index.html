Common Questions

Do I need to be in Spain to apply for the Digital Nomad Visa?

Yes, for the 3-year permit. While you can apply for a 1-year visa from a consulate abroad, we strongly recommend entering Spain as a tourist and applying directly for the 3-year residency permit via the UGE-CE in Madrid. It is faster and grants longer stability.

What is the minimum income requirement for 2026?

For the Digital Nomad Visa, you must prove 200% of the SMI (Minimum Interprofessional Wage). In 2026, this amounts to approximately ‚Ç¨2,700 per month for a single applicant, with additions for spouses and children.

Can JuroSpain guarantee my approval?

No honest lawyer can guarantee the decision of a government official. However, we offer a Zero Rejection Record guarantee on files we pre-validate. If we say your file is ready, it gets approved. If we doubt it, we won't let you file until it is fixed.

How long does the process take?

Once we submit your file to the UGE-CE, they have a statutory limit of 20 working days to respond. If they fail to do so, your visa is approved by "Administrative Silence." Realistically, most of our clients receive their resolution within 15-25 days.
