The Public Deed of Sale (Title Deed). The final legal document signed at the Notary that transfers ownership.

A public official required to certify deeds and contracts. They ensure legality but do not protect the buyer's interests like a lawyer does.

The administrative value of the property set by the Catastro. Usually much lower than market value; used to calculate IBI.

Community of Owners (HOA). The entity managing common areas (pool, gardens) in an apartment complex.

A local tax on the increase in land value. By law, this is paid by the seller.

A registered Real Estate Agent. While not mandatory in all regions, API agents are officially qualified.

Annual Property Tax (Council Tax). Calculated on Valor Catastral. Paid once a year to the Town Hall.

Tax paid by buyers of resale properties. Varies by region (6-10%). Replaces VAT.

Tax on documented legal acts (mortgages, new build deeds). Usually 0.5% - 1.5%.
